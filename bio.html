Rachel Moore

Originally from Wales, Uk, Rachel Moore enjoys learning and continuously strives to be the best at whatever she does. Moore spent the first half of her working life in New York City working for ad agencies and printing companies. She has a superior level of mastery in typography and photo retouching as seen in her Tiffany & Co. catalogs, and InStyle magazine covers.

Moore currently teaches Adobe video, Digital Design and Animation and Game art and Design at Guilford County schools. She is a lifelong learner. In her spare time she enjoys painting, designing and constructing with wood and concrete. As a world traveler, Moore has visited over 30 countries, lived in southeast Asia and speaks fluent Portuguese. She lives in Greensboro, NC with her two children and dog.
