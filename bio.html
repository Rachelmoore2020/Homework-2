Rachel Moore

I have always enjoyed the technical aspects of design and exploring web development is the logical next step for me. I have a growth mindset and I seek challenges often, whether it be learning to code, building a deck or learning how to install solar panels.



Originally from Wales, Uk, Rachel Moore continuously strives to be the best at what she does.

Moore spent the first half of her working life in New York City working for ad agencies and printing companies. Some highlights being retouching and typography for Tiffany & Co., and photo retouching for InStyle and People magazine.

Moore currently teaches Adobe video, Digital Design and Animation and Game art and Design at Guilford County schools. She is a lifelong learner. In her spare time she enjoys painting, designing and constructing with wood and concrete, and watching youtube to learn. As a world traveler, Moore has visited over 30 countries, lived in southeast Asia and speaks fluent Portuguese. She lives in Greensboro, NC with her two children and dog.
